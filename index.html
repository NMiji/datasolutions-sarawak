About Us

Data Solutions (Sarawak) Sdn Bhd

Data Solutions (Sarawak) Sdn Bhd is a branch office of Data Solutions Sdn Bhd situated in Kuching Sarawak.

  • Our team is comprised of programming and data processing experts who are passionate about their work and are equipped with extensive knowledge in a wide variety of applications.
  • We offer university students an opportunity to join our Internship programme in a career field of their choice, allowing students to gain the experience and professional skills required to advance in their dream career.

Our Services

Programming

Our team is capable of developing and maintaining organization's systems software and computing infrastructure with superior problem solving and critical thinking skills in achieving increased efficiency and service.

Document Processing

Partnering with The Legal Review Sdn Bhd, our skilled team provide conversion services of large volumes of legal documents into XML formats with self-quality check.

We provide a wide range of custom software development services. Our services cover the full software development life cycle. From pre-launch development, prototyping, code deployment and testing through to post-launch maintenance and support.
